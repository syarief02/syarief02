Powder · Tablet · Capsule Solvent: MeOH / H₂O SPE Busc Config
🔬CiQ samples: 10 mL sample + 10 mL H₂O → sonicate → measure pH → filter. Uses vacuum manifold with back-vessel insert.

⚠ pH Critical

Filter solution before SPE loading
